Theory

What exaclty is motivation? What types of motivation can be distinguished?

Definition

There are a lot of different and slightly different definitions of motivation. Accoding to Kendra Cherry, motivation is the process that initiates, guides, and maintains goal-oriented behaviors (Kendra Cherry, 2020). In other words, it is the reason why people do things that lead to accomplishing certain goals. A person could spend time watching TV, but for some reason he spent it writting a book, why? Because of motivation.

Types of motivation

People can be motivated to do things because of different reasons, based on where there reasons came from, phychologists distinguish two types of motivation:

  1. Extrinsic motivation. When person is extrinsicly motivated, he will do the work because it will lead him to some sort of result - money, awards, or work result itself. Person might not enjoy what he is doing, but he will do it, for external reasons. (David Burkus, 2020)
  2. Intrinsic motivation. That is when person does work because he enjoys doing it. It comes from the inside of a body, not from an external motivating factor. (David Burkus, 2020)
